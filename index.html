Hi, I'm Sydney — a certified teacher with a Master's degree in Early Childhood Education from Johns Hopkins University. I've spent over a decade helping young children learn to read, speak English, and build lifelong learning skills.

I use a direct instruction approach combined with hands-on, play-based learning — because the best readers are made through both structure and joy. I've also taught my own two children (ages 4 and 6) to read, so I truly understand both sides of this journey.
